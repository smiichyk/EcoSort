Our Goals

This website is designed to help you understand the basics of recycling at home.

Many people want to recycle but aren’t sure what can and cannot go into the recycling bin. Our goal is to provide simple, clear guidance that will make recycling easier for you and help protect the environment by reducing waste.

Together, we can improve recycling habits and make a positive impact on the planet.
